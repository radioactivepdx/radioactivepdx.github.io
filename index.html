A support group for humans who breathe air, drink water or eat food in Portland, Oregon.

Portland is contaminated with nuclear radiation, and it's getting worse.

What are we going to do about it?

To answer that question, and to take action, we created Radioactive Portland.

News Update 10/18/2013 21:30

We just launched a few hours ago, so things are messy. This fledgling website just hosts the discussion group right now, but there's more to come.

We'll be adding sections to match Radioactive Portland's three drivers:

  • Monitoring - Test results for background, rainwater, soil, food, milk
  • Knowledge - Articles and videos on radiation, detection, ecology, health
  • Action - Political, legal and commercial actions; calendar of events; trip reports

Our first order of business is to figure out when and where to meet up.
